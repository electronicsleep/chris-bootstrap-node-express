Linux

I would say my favorite thing about Linux is programming on the platform. Recently I have been learning graphics with SDL2/OpenGL and different graphics under Linux. I graviated towards the platform due to the command line and the vast information about programming and knowledge available.

I use Debian mostly and sometimes Ubuntu and CentOS due to the stablity of rpm based distro. I started off with slackware back in the day. Recent programming languages: Python, Java, PHP, Node.js, Perl, Bash, C++.

In 2016 I built a affordable and powerful Debian/GNU Linux desktop computer for audio recording, games and 3D graphics, thought I would share my hardware info. Everything works well with Linux.
