Linux Tower with Nvidia GPU

In 2016 I built a affordable and powerful Debian/GNU Linux desktop computer for audio recording, games and 3D graphics, thought I would share my hardware info. Everything works well with Linux.

I use Ardour for audio recording on Linux for my stuido and Blender, Krita and Gimp for artwork. Steam works really well and there are many great Linux games.

The M-Audio Delta PCI card is not new but also is known for a very stable reliable card.

M-Audio Delta PCI (DELTA 1010LT) Sound Card
